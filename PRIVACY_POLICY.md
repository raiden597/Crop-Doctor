# Privacy Policy — Sasya AI

**Last updated: May 2026**

## What we collect

Sasya AI does not collect, store, or share any personal data on our servers.

## How the app works

- **Images** you take or select are sent directly to the AI model (Google Gemini via OpenRouter) for analysis and are not stored by us.
- **API key** you enter is stored locally on your device only using Android's SharedPreferences. It is never transmitted to our servers.
- **Scan history** is stored locally on your device only. We cannot access it.

## Third-party services

The app uses **OpenRouter** (openrouter.ai) to run AI analysis. When you scan a crop, your image is sent to OpenRouter's servers. Please refer to [OpenRouter's Privacy Policy](https://openrouter.ai/privacy) for how they handle data.

## Permissions

- **Camera** — to photograph crops for analysis
- **Storage/Gallery** — to select existing photos for analysis
- **Internet** — to send images to the AI model for analysis

## Children's privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13.
